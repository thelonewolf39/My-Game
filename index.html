Welcome to Thryndor

Thryndor is an experimental sandbox RPG project in development. Explore worlds, craft tools, and survive in diverse biomes.

Game Features

  • Procedural terrain generation
  • Multiple biomes (dirt, sand, and more)
  • Crafting system with workbench logic
  • Player movement, jumping, and survival mechanics
